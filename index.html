Article 4: Updating Billing Information

  1. Verify Admin/Permission Status: Confirm you have Super Admin or Manage payments and subscriptions permissions in your HubSpot account, as only these users can update payment details.
  2. Go to Click your account name in the top right, then select Account & Billing.
  3. Go to the Payment Methods tab.
  4. Review and Update the Card: Check the current payment method for any typos in the card number, expiration date, or billing address. If you are certain the card details are correct, delete and re-add the card to force a fresh authorization with the payment processor.
  5. Contact Your Bank/ Contact HubSpot Billing(External Step): Call your bank or credit card company to confirm if they are blocking a charge from "HubSpot" or "Adyen" (HubSpot's payment processor) and authorize the transaction. If the card is confirmed as active and authorized by your bank, email the HubSpot Billing Team at billing@hubspot.com for further assistance with the payment attempt.

Article 5: Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  1. Navigate to Account Settings → Security.
  2. Select Enable 2FA.
  3. Choose SMS or Authenticator App.
  4. Scan the QR code using your authentication app.
  5. Enter the code to verify and activate 2FA.

4. TechFlow Knowledge Base Style Guide

Applies to

All TechFlow documentation (internal and customer-facing)

Purpose

To ensure clarity, consistency, and usability across all Knowledge Base articles and documents.

Voice & Tone

  • Voice: Friendly, professional, confident (not overly formal).
  • Tone: Supportive and solution-oriented.
  • Audience: Customers and internal staff.
  • Avoid: Slang, undefined abbreviations, robotic phrasing.

Structure of Every KB Article

  1. Overview
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Troubleshooting or FAQs
  4. Visuals
  5. Tips

Formatting Standards

  • Use H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections.
  • Bold for UI elements, italics for emphasis.
  • Numbered lists for steps, bullets for general points.
  • Include tables for data and error codes.
  • Active voice and present tense only.

Visual Guidelines

  • Use clear screenshots with captions and alt text.
  • Maintain brand colors: Blue, Gray, White.
  • Consistent icon styles and clean layouts.

Language & Grammar

  • Keep sentences short (under 20 words).
  • Start steps with action verbs.
  • Spell out acronyms once.
  • Use consistent product naming (TechFlow CRM).

Tagging & Metadata

Add tags, last updated date, owner, audience, and version info (e.g., #login #password #account).

Accessibility & Readability

  • Aim for 8th-grade reading level.
  • Use high contrast and descriptive alt text.
  • Avoid merged table cells.
  • Use descriptive link text (not “click here”).

Governance & Maintenance

  • Review articles quarterly and approve before publishing.
  • Audit outdated content twice yearly.
  • Track feedback and version history.

Templates & Naming Conventions

  • Title: Start with action words (e.g., “How to…”).
  • Folder: /KB/Category/Article_Title
  • Header: Title, version, date, owner.

Writing Tips for Agents

  • Think like the reader.
  • Start with the outcome.
  • Avoid assumptions.
  • Keep it scannable.
  • Test your own steps.
  • End with next steps.

Example Article

Title: How to Clear Browser Cache in TechFlow

Applies To: All TechFlow users

Owner: Support Team

Overview: Clearing your browser cache can resolve display or performance issues in TechFlow.

  1. Open Chrome Settings.
  2. Select “Privacy and Security”.
  3. Click “Clear Browsing Data”.
  4. Choose “Cached Images and Files”.
  5. Click “Clear Data”.

Troubleshooting: If pages still fail to load, restart your browser or check extensions.

Tip: Do this monthly to improve performance.
